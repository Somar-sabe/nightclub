About Us

Welcome to mar sharbel club, the ultimate destination for those seeking an unforgettable nightlife experience in the heart of Dubai. Our club offers a luxurious and exclusive atmosphere that is unmatched in the city, where you can relax and unwind in a peaceful environment.

We pride ourselves on providing a hygienic and safe place for our guests, as well as hosting some of the biggest Arabic stars and live performances. Our professional Arabic DJs will keep you dancing all night long with the best beats and music.

At mar sharbel club, we offer a wide variety of live entertainment, including dancing shows and aerial acrobats, making it the perfect place to spend a night out with your friends. Our classy and mysterious ambiance is something you shouldn’t miss.
